# Privacy policy

slopscore collects no data. There is nothing to opt out of.

- Everything runs locally: the CLI, the git hooks, the GitHub Action and
  the Claude Code plugin score your text in-process on your machine (or
  your CI runner). No LLM, no network calls, no telemetry, no analytics.
- The text you score - commit messages, PR bodies, files, diffs - is never
  transmitted anywhere, stored anywhere, or seen by anyone but you. Reports
  are printed to your terminal and discarded.
- We operate no servers and hold no user data of any kind.
- Distribution platforms (PyPI, GitHub) handle downloads under their own
  privacy policies; what slopscore does after you install it is described
  above: nothing.

Questions: https://github.com/koopatroopa/slopscore/issues
